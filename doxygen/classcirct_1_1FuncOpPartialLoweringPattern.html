Detailed Description

FuncOpPartialLoweringPatterns are patterns which intend to match on FuncOps and then perform their own walking of the IR.

FuncOpPartialLoweringPatterns have direct access to the ComponentLoweringState for the corresponding component of the matched FuncOp.
